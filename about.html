About

At Premier Threads, we bring together the art of crochet with a passion for creativity and innovation. Founded by Raiza Mae Peresores, a BSIT student, this website serves as both a platform for showcasing unique, handmade crocheted items and a personal project aimed at bridging the worlds of craft and technology. Each product, from customizable keychains to beautiful flower bouquets, is meticulously crafted using high-quality materials, ensuring both beauty and durability. As a student, I’ve poured my heart into creating an online space that’s not only easy to navigate but also celebrates the handmade process. Thank you for supporting my journey—your purchase helps me grow both as a developer and a crafter!
